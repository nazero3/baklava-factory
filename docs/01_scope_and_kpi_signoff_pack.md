# Baklava Factory Scope & KPI Sign-Off Pack

## Purpose
This document is used with business stakeholders to confirm exactly what will be included in the first release and how success will be measured.

## Stakeholders and Decision Rights
- Business Owner: final scope and budget approval
- Operations Lead: production and warehouse process approval
- Accounting Lead: costing, margin, and reconciliation approval
- Store Lead (one representative for 3 stores): transfer, sales, and returns flow approval
- Project Manager: records decisions and tracks open items

## Scope Confirmation (Release 1: Mandatory)
The first release includes:
1. Supplier and purchasing management
2. Raw material receiving and inventory by batch/lot
3. Production orders with recipe-based consumption
4. Finished goods inventory by kilograms
5. Transfer to 3 stores with dispatch/receipt confirmation
6. Store stock movement (sale, return, waste)
7. Physical stock count and variance adjustment approvals
8. Daily/weekly management reports
9. Role-based access and transaction audit log
10. Import/export via Excel/CSV

## Out of Scope (Release 2 Candidate)
- Full POS replacement
- Advanced forecasting AI
- Mobile native app (web-mobile remains in scope)
- Multi-factory orchestration
- Full e-commerce integration

## KPI Definitions and Targets (First 12 Weeks After Go-Live)
1. Stock Accuracy
   - Formula: 1 - (absolute variance / book stock)
   - Target: >= 97%
2. Wastage Rate
   - Formula: total waste kg / total produced kg
   - Target: reduce by >= 15% from baseline
3. Transfer Completion Rate
   - Formula: confirmed transfers / dispatched transfers
   - Target: >= 99% same-day confirmation
4. Gross Margin Visibility
   - Formula: (sales - cost of goods sold) / sales
   - Target: daily visibility by product and store
5. Report Preparation Time
   - Formula: manual reporting hours per week
   - Target: reduce by >= 50%

## Business Rules to Approve
- Standard unit is kilogram; gram conversions are system-managed
- Every production order must reference an approved recipe version
- Every store receipt must reference a transfer note
- Costing method for release 1: weighted average cost per kg
- Adjustments above threshold require manager approval

## Sign-Off Checklist
- Scope list accepted by all 4 business stakeholders
- KPI formulas and targets accepted
- Release 1 and Release 2 boundary accepted
- Approval matrix accepted
- Open risks reviewed and accepted

## Workshop Agenda (90 minutes)
1. Process walk-through (20 min)
2. Scope validation (25 min)
3. KPI and reports validation (20 min)
4. Risks and assumptions (15 min)
5. Sign-off and action owners (10 min)

## Deliverables from This Step
- Signed scope baseline
- Signed KPI baseline sheet
- Signed approval matrix
- List of open items and owners
