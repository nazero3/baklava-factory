# Baklava Factory End-to-End Solution
## Executive Proposal (Cloud and On-Premise)

## What the Solution Covers
- Track all incoming raw materials
- Manage production conversion to baklava by kilograms
- Track transfer to three stores
- Monitor sales, returns, waste, and stock differences
- Provide daily cost and margin visibility

## Two Deployment Choices
1. Cloud (online): faster rollout, lower upfront cost, easier scaling
2. On-premise (offline/company-only): full local control, higher infrastructure effort

## What Makes Either Option Valid
- Clear ownership and process governance
- Accurate master data and recipe setup
- Strong access control and audit logging
- Backup and disaster recovery process
- User training and adoption plan
- KPI-based performance measurement

## Implementation Timeline
- Cloud-first: 12-16 weeks
- On-premise: 14-20 weeks
