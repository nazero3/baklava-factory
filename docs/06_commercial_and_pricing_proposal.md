# Commercial and Pricing Proposal
## Baklava Factory Management Solution (Syrian Market)

## Pricing Philosophy
- Keep the entry point affordable
- Separate mandatory scope from optional enhancements
- Use milestone billing to reduce customer risk
- Use USD contract with invoice-day SYP conversion to handle FX volatility

## Option A: Cloud (Recommended Base)
### One-Time Implementation
- USD 24,000 to USD 34,000

### Recurring Cost
- Hosting and managed support: USD 450 to USD 1,100 per month

### Training
- Basic onboarding included
- Advanced training package: USD 1,500 to USD 3,000

### Why This Is Commercially Attractive
- Lower startup cost
- Faster time to value
- Predictable monthly operations cost

## Option B: On-Premise (Premium Control Option)
### One-Time Implementation
- USD 28,000 to USD 42,000

### Infrastructure
- Server, UPS, NAS, firewall/VPN: USD 6,000 to USD 15,000

### Annual Support
- 15% to 22% of implementation value

### Why This Is Chosen by Some Customers
- Maximum local data control
- Better fit for strict internal governance environments

## Suggested Packaging
### Package 1: Core Operations (Best Entry)
- Procurement + inventory + production + transfer + core reports

### Package 2: Core + Finance Insights
- Package 1 plus margin dashboards and advanced costing analytics

### Package 3: Full Optimization
- Package 2 plus integration add-ons (POS/accounting), approvals mobility, advanced BI

## Suggested Payment Milestones
- 20% at project kickoff
- 25% at design sign-off
- 30% after UAT completion
- 15% at go-live
- 10% after hypercare closure

## Proposed Commercial Terms to Increase Acceptance
- Fixed-scope release 1 with transparent change-request policy
- 60-90 day post-go-live support commitment
- Optional managed service contract after stabilization

## Return-on-Investment Narrative for Customer
- Lower stock loss and over-purchasing
- Lower waste and better yield discipline
- Faster management decisions from daily visibility
- Reduced manual reporting burden
- Better store-level margin control
