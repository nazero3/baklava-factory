# MVP Showcase Demo Day Script

## Audience
- Customer owner/decision makers
- Operations, warehouse, production, stores, accounting

## Duration
- 75 to 90 minutes

## Demo Agenda
1. Business problem and MVP objective (5 min)
2. Live process demonstration (50 min)
3. KPI and management reports (15 min)
4. Q&A and decision capture (15-20 min)

## Live Demonstration Sequence

## Part A: Inbound (10 min)
- Show supplier receiving with lot and cost
- Show immediate stock update in raw inventory

## Part B: Production (15 min)
- Show production order creation from recipe
- Show material consumption and finished output
- Show waste capture

## Part C: Distribution (10 min)
- Dispatch transfer from factory to store
- Confirm receipt at store side
- Show stock movement across locations

## Part D: Store Operations (10 min)
- Enter daily sales movement and returns
- Perform end-of-day reconciliation

## Part E: Management View (15 min)
- Open dashboard:
  - Stock status
  - Waste trend
  - Cost per kg
  - Margin by product/store
- Show one audit log example

## Demo Data Checklist
- At least 5 raw materials
- At least 3 baklava products
- At least 2 active recipes
- At least 1 transfer per store
- At least 2 days of store movement data

## What to Highlight to Customer
- Ease of use for non-technical users
- Full traceability from receiving to sale
- Faster decision-making via daily KPIs
- Reduction of manual spreadsheet dependency
